在前端开发中使用ANT

ant作为强大的构建工具，贯穿了开发的编译、打包、测试、部署的过程。对于前端开发也可以使用ant来构建！

http://github.com/jquery/jquery/blob/master/build.xml

上面是jQuery的build.xml文件。jQuery使用了ant实现了打包、测试的过程。

[xml]<target name="all" depends="jquery,lint,min" />[/xml]

jQuery的执行ant时，会先执行jquery target，然后是lint target，最后min target。

执行jquery target时，首先会执行init和selector target：

[xml] <target name="qunit-clone" unless="qunit">
<exec executable="git" outputproperty="git-qunit" >
<arg line="clone git://github.com/jquery/qunit.git test/qunit" />
</exec>
<echo message="git clone qunit: ${git-qunit}" />
</target>
<target name="qunit-pull" if="qunit">
<exec executable="git" outputproperty="git-qunit" dir="test/qunit" >
<arg line="pull origin master" />
</exec>
<echo message="git pull sizzle: ${git-qunit}" />
</target>
<target name="sizzle-clone" unless="sizzle">
<exec executable="git" outputproperty="git-sizzle" >
<arg line="clone git://github.com/jeresig/sizzle.git src/sizzle" />
</exec>
<echo message="git clone sizzle: ${git-sizzle}" />
</target>
<target name="sizzle-pull" if="sizzle">
<exec executable="git" outputproperty="git-sizzle" dir="src/sizzle" >
<arg line="pull origin master" />
</exec>
<echo message="git pull sizzle: ${git-sizzle}" />
</target>

&lt;target name=&quot;init&quot; depends=&quot;qunit-clone,qunit-pull,sizzle-clone,sizzle-pull&quot; /&gt;

&lt;target name=&quot;selector&quot; depends=&quot;init&quot; description=&quot;Builds the selector library for jQuery from Sizzle.&quot;&gt;
    &lt;copy file=&quot;src/sizzle/sizzle.js&quot; tofile=&quot;src/selector.js&quot; overwrite=&quot;true&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;replaceregexp match=&quot;// EXPOSE(.*)&amp;#10;&quot; replace=&quot;// EXPOSE\1&amp;#10;${sizzle-exports}&quot; file=&quot;src/selector.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;replaceregexp match=&quot;window.Sizzle(.*)&amp;#10;&quot; replace=&quot;&quot; file=&quot;src/selector.js&quot; /&gt;
&lt;/target&gt;[/xml]
  • qunit-clone：从git-hub上clone qunit.js下来
  • qunit-pull：从git-hub上更新qunit.js
  • sizzle-clone：从git-hub上clone sizzle.js下来
  • sizzle-pull：从git-hub上更新sizzle.js

  • selector：拷贝sizzele.js到src文件夹并且替换其中的两个值
    [xml]<target name="jquery" depends="init,selector" description="Main jquery build, concatenates source files and replaces @VERSION">

    &lt;echo message=&quot;Building ${JQ}&quot; /&gt;
&lt;mkdir dir=&quot;${dist}&quot; /&gt;
&lt;concat destfile=&quot;${JQ}&quot;&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/intro.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/core.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/support.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/data.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/queue.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/attributes.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/event.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/selector.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/traversing.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/manipulation.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/css.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/ajax.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/effects.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/offset.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/dimensions.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/outro.js&quot; /&gt;
&lt;/concat&gt;
&lt;replaceregexp match=&quot;@VERSION&quot; replace=&quot;${version}&quot; flags=&quot;g&quot; byline=&quot;true&quot; file=&quot;${JQ}&quot; /&gt;
&lt;exec executable=&quot;git&quot; outputproperty=&quot;date&quot;&gt;
    &lt;arg line=&quot;log -1 --pretty=format:%ad&quot; /&gt;
&lt;/exec&gt;
&lt;replaceregexp match=&quot;Date: &quot; replace=&quot;Date: ${date}&quot; file=&quot;${JQ}&quot; /&gt;
&lt;echo message=&quot;${JQ} built.&quot; /&gt;

    </target>

    <target name="lint" depends="jquery" description="Check jQuery against JSLint">

    &lt;exec executable=&quot;java&quot;&gt;
    &lt;arg line=&quot;-jar build/js.jar build/jslint-check.js&quot; /&gt;
&lt;/exec&gt;

    </target>[/xml]

从上面的代码可以知道：jquery目标实现的src文件夹下的文件的连接（利用了concat）。并且会替换version信息和Date日期。

[xml]<target name="lint" depends="jquery" description="Check jQuery against JSLint">
<exec executable="java">
<arg line="-jar build/js.jar build/jslint-check.js" />
</exec>
</target>[/xml]

lint target所作的事情就是利用jslint检查jQuery.js。

下一个target是min：

[xml] <target name="min" depends="jquery" description="Remove all comments and whitespace, no compression, great in combination with GZip">
<echo message="Building ${JQ_MIN}" />
<apply executable="java" parallel="false" verbose="true" dest="${dist}">
<fileset dir="${dist}">
<include name="jquery.js" />
</fileset>
<arg line="-jar" />
<arg path="build/google-compiler-20091218.jar" />
<arg value="—warning_level" />
<arg value="QUIET" />
<arg value="—js_output_file" />
<targetfile />
<arg value="—js" />
<mapper type="glob" from="jquery.js" to="tmpmin" />
</apply>
<concat destfile="${JQ_MIN}">
<filelist files="${JQ}, ${dist}/tmpmin" />
<filterchain>
<headfilter lines="15" />
</filterchain>
</concat>
<concat destfile="${JQ_MIN}" append="yes">
<filelist files="${dist}/tmpmin" />
</concat>
<delete file="${dist}/tmpmin" />
<echo message="${JQ_MIN} built." />
</target>[/xml]

很明显，min做的事情就是使用google-compiler来编译jquery.js文件，生成jquery.min.js文件。
另外还有其他的target：clean和openAjaxMetadata。
clean做的事情就是清理工作；
openAjaxMetadata做的事情就是描述就是生成OpenAjax的metadata（没有细究，不确定）；
仿照jquery的写法，我自己在使用ant时的build.xml是：

[xml]<project name="MZhou" default="all" basedir=".">

&lt;loadfile property=&quot;version&quot; srcfile=&quot;version.txt&quot; /&gt;
&lt;property name=&quot;PREFIX&quot; value=&quot;.&quot; /&gt;
&lt;property description=&quot;Folder for mzhou and min target&quot; name=&quot;dist&quot; value=&quot;${PREFIX}/dist&quot; /&gt;

&lt;property name=&quot;MZ&quot; value=&quot;${dist}/mzhou.js&quot; /&gt;
&lt;property name=&quot;MZCSS&quot; value=&quot;${dist}/mzhou.css&quot; /&gt;
&lt;property name=&quot;MZ_MIN&quot; value=&quot;${dist}/mzhou.min.js&quot; /&gt;
&lt;property name=&quot;MZCSS_MIN&quot; value=&quot;${dist}/mzhou.css&quot; /&gt;

&lt;target name=&quot;all&quot; depends=&quot;build,lint,min&quot; /&gt;

&lt;!--构建--&gt;
&lt;target name=&quot;build&quot; depends=&quot;init,selector&quot; description=&quot;Main mzhou build, concatenates source files and replaces @VERSION&quot;&gt;
    &lt;echo message=&quot;Building ${MZ}&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;mkdir dir=&quot;${dist}&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;concat destfile=&quot;${MZ}&quot;&gt;
        &lt;!-- 按顺序放置 --&gt;
        &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/intro.js&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/core.js&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;!-- ...... --&gt;
    &lt;/concat&gt;
    &lt;concat destfile=&quot;${MZCSS}&quot;&gt;
        &lt;!-- 按顺序放置 --&gt;
        &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/intro.css&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;fileset file=&quot;src/core.css&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;!-- ...... --&gt;
    &lt;/concat&gt;
    &lt;replaceregexp match=&quot;@VERSION&quot; replace=&quot;${version}&quot; flags=&quot;g&quot; byline=&quot;true&quot; file=&quot;${MZ}&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;exec executable=&quot;git&quot; outputproperty=&quot;date&quot;&gt;
        &lt;arg line=&quot;log -1 --pretty=format:%ad&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;/exec&gt;
    &lt;replaceregexp match=&quot;Date: &quot; replace=&quot;Date: ${date}&quot; file=&quot;${MZ}&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;echo message=&quot;${MZ} built.&quot; /&gt;
&lt;/target&gt;

&lt;!-- jslint语法检查 --&gt;
&lt;target name=&quot;lint&quot; depends=&quot;mzhou&quot; description=&quot;Check jQuery against JSLint&quot;&gt;
    &lt;exec executable=&quot;java&quot;&gt;
        &lt;arg line=&quot;-jar build/js.jar build/jslint-check.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;/exec&gt;
&lt;/target&gt;

&lt;!-- 压缩 --&gt;
&lt;target name=&quot;min&quot; depends=&quot;mzhou&quot; description=&quot;Remove all comments and whitespace, no compression, great in combination with GZip&quot;&gt;
    &lt;!-- 压缩js --&gt;
    &lt;echo message=&quot;Building ${MZ_MIN} js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;apply executable=&quot;java&quot; parallel=&quot;false&quot; verbose=&quot;true&quot; dest=&quot;${dist}&quot;&gt;
        &lt;fileset dir=&quot;${dist}&quot;&gt;
            &lt;include name=&quot;mzhou.js&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;/fileset&gt;
        &lt;arg line=&quot;-jar&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;arg path=&quot;build/google-compiler-20091218.jar&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;arg value=&quot;--warning_level&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;arg value=&quot;QUIET&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;arg value=&quot;--js_output_file&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;targetfile /&gt;
        &lt;arg value=&quot;--js&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;mapper type=&quot;glob&quot; from=&quot;mzhou.js&quot; to=&quot;tmpmin&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;/apply&gt;
    &lt;concat destfile=&quot;${MZ_MIN}&quot;&gt;
        &lt;filelist files=&quot;${MZ}, ${dist}/tmpmin&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;filterchain&gt;
            &lt;headfilter lines=&quot;15&quot; /&gt;
        &lt;/filterchain&gt;
    &lt;/concat&gt;
    &lt;concat destfile=&quot;${MZ_MIN}&quot; append=&quot;yes&quot;&gt;
        &lt;filelist files=&quot;${dist}/tmpmin&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;/concat&gt;
    &lt;delete file=&quot;${dist}/tmpmin&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;echo message=&quot;${MZ_MIN} built.&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;!-- 压缩css --&gt;
    &lt;echo message=&quot;Building ${MZCSS_MIN} css&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;apply executable=&quot;java&quot;  dest=&quot;${dist}&quot;  failonerror=&quot;true&quot;  parallel=&quot;false&quot; &gt;
        &lt;fileset dir=&quot;${dist}&quot; includes=&quot;mzhou.css&quot;/&gt;
        &lt;arg line=&quot;-jar&quot;/&gt;
        &lt;arg path=&quot;build/yuicompressor.jar&quot;/&gt;
        &lt;arg line=&quot;--charset utf-8&quot;/&gt;
        &lt;srcfile/&gt;
        &lt;arg line=&quot;-o&quot;/&gt;
        &lt;targetfile/&gt;
        &lt;mapper type=&quot;regexp&quot; from=&quot;mzhou.css&quot; to=&quot;${MZCSS_MIN}&quot;/&gt;
    &lt;/apply&gt;
    &lt;echo message=&quot;${MZCSS_MIN} built.&quot; /&gt;
&lt;/target&gt;

&lt;target name=&quot;clean&quot;&gt;
    &lt;delete dir=&quot;${dist}&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;delete file=&quot;src/selector.js&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;delete dir=&quot;test/qunit&quot; /&gt;
    &lt;delete dir=&quot;src/sizzle&quot; /&gt;
&lt;/target&gt;

</project>[/xml]
